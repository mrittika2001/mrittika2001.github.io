  • Project

    The primary objective of this project was to examine the prevalence of age heaping and age misreporting tendencies in the data of the 2011 census, with a specific focus on the state of Bihar. Through the study, it was discovered that Bihar exhibited the highest Myres blended index and adult dependency ratio, coupled with the lowest literacy rate. On the other hand, Lakshadweep had the lowest Myres blended index, while Daman and Diu had the lowest dependency ratio, and Kerala had the highest literacy rate. Furthermore, the study revealed a negative correlation between literacy rate and Myres blended index, as well as dependency ratio. However, conclusive findings could not be drawn with regards to the relationship between literacy rate and age-sex accuracy.
